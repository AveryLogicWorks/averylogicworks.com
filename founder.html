Why this exists

Independent work deserves a real home.

This studio exists to turn research, software ideas, and careful system design into actual releases. Some work will be public early. Some will stay private until timing, structure, and protection are right.

The public site is intentionally selective. It gives people enough to understand the mission without giving away everything before it is time.

Why support helps

Support reduces drag and increases forward motion.

Early support helps cover hosting, tooling, experimentation, infrastructure, and development time. It also helps protect the pace of the work from being dictated by short-term constraints.
