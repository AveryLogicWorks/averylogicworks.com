Privacy & email preferences

Small, understandable privacy language.

This page is designed to be readable, simple, and realistic for an early-stage founder-led site.

What may be collected

  • Name or display name
  • Email address
  • Newsletter and supporter-email preferences
  • Basic transaction and support records when support or billing is involved

How it may be used

  • To send requested updates or newsletters
  • To manage supporter communications and perks
  • To respond to support, billing, or dispute questions
  • To improve the website and future releases

Opting out

Users should be able to opt out through the footer unsubscribe link in a live newsletter or through the website settings page when account sync is live.

The site shell already includes an account settings page for newsletter and supporter-email toggles.

Contact

Privacy or support questions can be directed to support@averylogicworks.com.
