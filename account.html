Account settings

Simple settings, not a giant preference maze.

This page keeps settings intentionally small: newsletters on/off, supporter emails on/off, and an account lock state shell for later live use.

Preferences

Email and account controls

How this is meant to work

Two categories are enough to start.

  • Newsletters: broad updates, drops, and major announcements
  • Supporter emails: donation-related follow-ups, perk notices, and supporter-only admin messages
  • Lock state: a front-end placeholder until your live auth system handles it for real

This page currently saves to local browser storage so you can preview the experience before wiring a backend.
